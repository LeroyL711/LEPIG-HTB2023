Welcome to planIt!


We understand how frustrating it can be to keep track of job applications, especially when you're applying to multiple jobs at once. That's why we created planIt - to help job seekers like you stay organized and on top of their applications.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to simplify the job search process and make it easier for you to find your next opportunity. With planIt, you can easily track the jobs you've applied to, along with important details like the application deadline, the company name, and the job title.

What is out value?

We started PlanIt because we know firsthand how overwhelming the job search process can be. Our team has been in your shoes, and we understand the frustration of sending out countless applications and then struggling to keep track of them all. That's why we've created a platform that is user-friendly and easy to use, allowing you to focus on what really matters - finding the right job for you.

What makes us different?

At planIt, we're committed to providing you with the best possible experience. We're always looking for ways to improve our platform and make it even more helpful for job seekers. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Thank you for choosing planIt. We're here to help you succeed in your job search!
